The Junior college is HAAC accredited ‘A’ grade by the Maharashtra State Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education and enjoys the status of Gujarati Linguistic Minority Institution.

The college has fully qualified and experienced staff members and is counted among one of the best Commerce colleges in Mumbai.

The regular activities of the college include regular PTA meetings, compulsory attendance norms, activities like celebration of Hindi Divas, Marathi Divas, Talent and Hobby Exhibition and various Sports and Cultural activities, fostering the all round development of Students.

Regular elocution competitions, Debates, Mehandi and Rangoli competitions, etc are held throughout the year.
