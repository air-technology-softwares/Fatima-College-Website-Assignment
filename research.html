Our institution has always laid stress on quality research, and we want to make sure we incorporate a proper framework to facilitate that.

Main objectives of the research committee are to promote research activities of faculty members and students, to train the students about research procedures and formalities, to collaborate with other research centres and colleges, to organize workshops on research methodology, how to select research topics, how to go for literature review etc. and to mentor the students on how to make research papers.
